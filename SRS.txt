## **1. Introduction**

### **1.1 Purpose**

The purpose of this SRS document is to define the requirements for the **Fertilizer Inventory Management System (FIMS)**.
The system manages the **flow of material** from the **Rake Point** to multiple **Warehouses**, and from there to **Dealers**, **Government departments**, and **Payal Fertilizers (own company)**.
It automates **inventory tracking, dispatch, billing, and rent/lifting charge calculations** for an end-to-end supply chain of fertilizers.

---

### **1.2 Scope**

The system handles:

* Management of **rake unloading** and **distribution**.
* **Stock monitoring** across **three warehouses** and one **rake point**.
* **Dispatch management** to dealers, own company, or government.
* **Billing system** for transportation, rent, and lifting charges.
* **Automated reports** for monthly dispatches, warehouse stock, and pending bills.

The goal is to ensure **accurate, real-time visibility** of fertilizer stock and simplify coordination between **suppliers, warehouse operators, dispatchers, and managers**.

---

### **1.3 Intended Users**

| Role                           | Description                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Admin / Manager**            | Oversees all operations, adds new rake entries, assigns stock to warehouses or dispatches, and monitors reports. |
| **Warehouse Worker**           | Records rake unloading details, manages stock in/out, and updates storage info.                                  |
| **Dispatch Clerk**             | Handles truck dispatches to dealers, Payal Fertilizers, and Government. Generates transportation bills.          |
| **Billing & Accounts Officer** | Calculates rent for leftover stock, lifting charges, and generates invoices.                                     |
| **Dealer / Company User**      | Views dispatch records, pending deliveries, and billing details.                                                 |

---

## **2. Overall Description**

### **2.1 System Environment**

* The system will be a **web-based application**, accessible on desktop and tablet devices.
* Backend will be connected to a **centralized database** managing all warehouses, rakes, and dispatch data.

### **2.2 System Objectives**

1. Maintain **real-time stock data** at all locations.
2. Automate **dispatch & billing operations**.
3. Provide **transparent and trackable** movement from rake point to final delivery.
4. Support **multi-company & multi-location** configurations.

---

## **3. System Features**

### **3.1 Rake Management Module**

**Purpose:** Manage incoming rakes from multiple companies.

**Features:**

* Admin adds rake details:

  * Rake Number, Date, Supplier Company
  * Quantity (in MT)
  * Destination allocation (Warehouse or Market Dispatch)
* System divides stock among warehouses or market dispatch.
* Warehouse user enters **unloading details** (e.g., actual received MT, shortages).

---

### **3.2 Warehouse Management Module**

**Purpose:** Handle stock at 3 warehouse locations.

**Features:**

* Record stock in (from rake) and stock out (to dealers/company).
* Maintain per-lot tracking with:

  * Quantity in storage
  * Rent start date
  * Storage section details
* Generate alerts for **old stock pending beyond threshold**.
* Generate **warehouse-wise stock reports**.

---

### **3.3 Market Dispatch Module**

**Purpose:** Manage direct dispatches from rake point to customers.

**Features:**

* Record direct dispatch to:

  * Dealers
  * Payal Fertilizers (own company)
  * Government orders
* Generate transportation bills for each dispatch.
* Maintain **truck details**, **dispatch date**, and **quantity sent**.

---

### **3.4 Billing & Rent Module**

**Purpose:** Automate billing for transport, rent, and lifting.

**Features:**

* Calculate **transportation bills** for market dispatches.
* Calculate **rent** for stock stored in warehouse:

  * Company and Dealers pay per day per MT.
* Calculate **lifting charges** when stock is dispatched from warehouse.
* Generate **monthly summaries** and **exportable invoices** (PDF/Excel).

---

### **3.5 Reporting & Analytics Module**

**Purpose:** Provide visibility into stock and dispatch performance.

**Features:**

* Rake-wise, Warehouse-wise, and Dealer-wise reports.
* Summary dashboards for:

  * Total Rakes handled
  * Total stock dispatched and in-hand
  * Billing summary (Rent + Transport)
* Downloadable in PDF/Excel formats.

## **5. Functional Requirements**

| ID  | Function         | Description                                                   |
| --- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| FR1 | Add Rake         | Admin adds new rake with source and allocation details.       |
| FR2 | Record Unloading | Warehouse worker enters rake unloading data.                  |
| FR3 | Allocate Stock   | System distributes rake material across warehouses or market. |
| FR4 | Dispatch Stock   | Record truck dispatch and bill generation.                    |
| FR5 | Calculate Rent   | Rent auto-calculated for stock held beyond threshold days.    |
| FR6 | Generate Bills   | Create and export transport, rent, and lifting bills.         |
| FR7 | Generate Reports | Produce rake-wise and warehouse-wise summaries.               |

---

## **6. Non-Functional Requirements**

| Type            | Description                                                      |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Performance** | Must handle up to 100 concurrent users with <2s response time.   |
| **Scalability** | Should support addition of new warehouses or rake points easily. |
| **Security**    | Role-based authentication (Admin, Warehouse, Dispatch, Billing). |
| **Usability**   | Clean UI with minimal data entry fields per screen.              |
| **Backup**      | Daily backup of data with cloud sync.                            |
